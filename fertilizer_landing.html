Export Operations
How We Load & Ship
Every shipment from LTM AGRI follows a rigorous, internationally supervised export protocol — from product analysis to container sealing.

At LTM AGRI, every export operation is conducted under the full supervision of internationally recognized inspection companies — SGS or Intertek — guaranteeing that what leaves our facility matches exactly what was agreed in the contract, in terms of quantity, quality, and condition.

Before and during each loading operation, we conduct a comprehensive product analysis through accredited international laboratories. Every batch receives a Certificate of Analysis (COA) confirming nutrient content, moisture levels, granulometry, and heavy metal compliance — valid for port acceptance in 100+ countries.

All our products are loaded in bulk. Prior to loading, each container is thoroughly cleaned and inspected. The entire inner surface is then lined with heavy-duty plastic liner sheets, protecting the product from moisture, contamination, and container walls — ensuring product integrity from loading to destination port.

✅ SGS Supervised ✅ Intertek Certified ✅ Intl. Lab Analysis ✅ Bulk Loading ✅ Plastic-Lined Containers ✅ Full Documentation
1
Product Sampling & Lab Analysis

Each batch is sampled and tested by an accredited international laboratory before loading begins. COA issued.

2
Container Inspection & Cleaning

All containers are inspected for structural integrity, odors, and contamination. Cleaned and certified fit for food-grade cargo.

3
Plastic Liner Installation

Heavy-duty plastic liner sheets cover the full inner surface of each container — floor, walls, and ceiling — before any product enters.

4
Supervised Bulk Loading by Conveyor

Product is loaded via conveyor belt under continuous SGS/Intertek supervision. Weight and flow are monitored in real time.

5
Sealing & Full Documentation

Containers are sealed, SGS/Intertek report issued. Full doc pack: COA, COO, Inspection Report, BL, Phytosanitary Cert.
